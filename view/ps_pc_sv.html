STATIVE VERBS

DO NOT USUALLY DESCRIBE ACTIONS. THEY DESCRIBE STATES(FEELINGS,THOUGTS…).THEY ARE NOT USUALLY USED IN PRESENT CONTINUOUS.
APPEAR-BE-BELIEVE-BELONG TO- HATE-HAVE-INCLUDE-KNOW- LIKE-LOVE-NEED-PREFER-SEE-SEEM-TASTE-THINK- UNDERSTAND-WANT.
SOME USED IN CONTINUOUS WHEN THEY DESCRIBE AN ACTION: I’M THINKING TO GIVE UP.
